2025.5.23
Nuisance Animals All Year Round!
2025
The Hague
exhibiting

This year I have the honor to be the Stadstekenaar Den Haag (City Illustrator of The Hague). Every month I make 2 drawings telling stories about the city's nuisance animals and the coexistence with human. Throughout the year me and Laura organise workshops with children to make sock puppets and mascots to embody these animals. You can go to the city library to see the XXL prints of the drawings. The print will be updated every 2~3 months.

From May 23 to June 14 the original drawings and the sock puppets are exhibited at NEST Den Haag.
