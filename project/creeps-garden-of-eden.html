Creep's Garden of Eden
2024
drawing
water color
color pencil
What do you see if you zoom in onto the canvas' corners of the classical depiction of Garden of Eden? You see insects, undergrowth, all kinds of crawlies creeping under the creatures above. This drawing imagines that these "creeps" are the main characters of Garden of Eden.

It is also an amplified impression of my life and friends at Jan van Eyck academy as I am, through immersing and inspiring myself day by day in the garden, embodying the horny fly, the ecstatic mouse, and, a pink little flower feeling so ready to squirt my pollen and then crunched dead by the snail on a same day!

The drawing has been adapted into a poster for the Jan van Eyck party under the series of "Dragana Bar" organised by Warsaww-based Collective Kem.
