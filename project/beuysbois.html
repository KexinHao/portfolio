BeuysBois Identity Design

identitylogo
Beuys Bois is a queer collectives founded by KABK students. The initiative has a network within the art world and connections with emerging (queer) artists. Beuys Bois is a long term project that functions at the intersection between art and community. The rogramme involves community driven art events, performances, exhibitions and envisioning modes of bringing people together.

My task is to design the logo to make the identity clear and appealing.
