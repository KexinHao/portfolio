Daniele Formica and I designed the campaign for 2023 Graduation Show and 2023 Open Day at Royal Academy of Art The Hague.

We facilitated a one-day drawing workshop for students to gather and draw collectively a centipede that could represent the indentity of their academy. This centipede then evolved into a butterfly for the Open Day campaign.
