Diary of Compost Creatures

2024

drawingwater colourcolour pencil

During my residency at Jan van Eyck academy, I was taking care of the compost bin. I was very interested in the behaviours of the decomposers and other creatures I found there, such as fruit flies, slugs, and springtails. These are the anthropomorphized portrayals of these three characters.
