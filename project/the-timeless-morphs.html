The Timeless Morphs: 3 Body Sessions

video essayresearchembodiment
The visual essay looks into the moving body and asks the question: what is a disciplined body and what is an undisciplined one? Through travelling among the personal and the collective body, and through patching together family footages and historical archives, the essay not only observes the body in motion, but also investigates the states of being disciplined and undisciplined. It proposes our physical body as a processual, moving archive, and raises the importance of remembering as a tool of empowerment.

The visual essay is commissioned by and published on ArtEZ studium generale, as part of their research program Body and Power(lessness).
