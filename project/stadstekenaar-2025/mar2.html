Den Haag Centraal publishes the drawings and stories behind
Every month, a new drawing for the Stadstekening will be published in Den Haag Centraal. For the announcement of the new Stadstekenaar, they featured an interview with me and showcased my first drawing across a full spread. In the interview, I discussed the artistic vision behind the project, its themes, my personal experiences as a resident of The Hague and the Netherlands, and my perspective as a multidisciplinary artist.
