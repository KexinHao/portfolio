Exhibition and Publication Launch
On March 13th, the exhibition and the publication launch of the Haagse Stadstekenaar 2025 opened at Huis van het Boek with my performance - a silverfish and a rat, debating about archives, appetite, and love, cry on each other’s shoulders. Thank you Yoeri Meessen (director Huis van het Boek) for giving the opening speech and Laurie Cluitmans (director NEST) for the Q&A session with the 2 character️s.

The exhibition is at the garden house and it goes on till April 12. Please go and have a look of the original drawings and buy the publication!
