TikTok Collective Dance
next
home
medium video
Collective dance in a lot of cases has a political connotation. A crowd of people, positioned systematically, move together under a certain set of rules. This practice and aesthetic promote the idea of collectivism. In the age of self media and the dominance of mobile short video App, everyone shows off themselves. Individualism is largely celebrated. But the fact that people follow the trends — especially on the video App TikTok, where they like to dance to the music with the same movements, has a common ground with collective dancing. By conjoining single clips of dance, the artist tries to make the “collective dance” possible again, in a virtual and expansive space.
